I am first year Phd student in Computer Science and Engineering department at Universty of Michigan. I am advised by Nikola Banovic. My research area is human-computer interaction(HCI). I am particularly interested in creating better interaction methods for Human-AI collaboration. I am currently working on understanding user behavior when interacting with artificial intelligence (AI) in order to identify potential interaction mechanisms for human-AI interaction. In this research project, we are looking at how to improve users' ability to detect errors and harms in AI systems. Because AI is not free of bias, discrimination, unreliability, safety concerns, and poor outcomes, and not all AI is capable of making decisions that result in the desired outcome for the user.
